Our rooms have beautiful sea and garden views, as they are distributed in three buildings – the main building that hosts the reception/bar area, the Mini Spa & Wellness Room and the restaurant, as well as two autonomous buildings.

The complex integrates beautifully in its green surroundings, natural, since it is build on the slope of a tiny hill and artificial luscious green gardens with olive trees, palm trees and many flowers.

The Olympic-sized pool outdoor pool is the perfect spot to enjoy your swimming and sunbathing under the Greek sunshine. The Anjali Palace features an adjacent children’s pool 80 cm deep, especially designed so as to ensure the safety of your children, so feel free to unwind next to them, relish a cool refreshment or enjoy a swim.

Sun beds & umbrellas are available free of charge.

Our pool bar, besides soft drinks, and other (alcoholic) beverages, offers a variety of snacks that will satisfy your appetite including burgers, salads and sandwiches.

If you prefer to stay more active, try one of our outdoor activities. Mini – Golf courses, Table tennis, giant chess and Connect 4 can be found next to the swimming pool.

If you have a car (own or rented) parking will be no problem. You can park your car at a very short distance from your room. at no extra charge. In addition, the Anjali Palace is the only hotel in Delhi that offers an electric vehicle charging station.
